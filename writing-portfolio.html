Chanse Campbell

Screenwriter – Film & Television

Chanse is a working class writer from Leeds, interested in exploring underdog stories with themes that often centre around identity and belonging. This year’s winner of Channel 4’s Writing for Television Awards (run by New Writing North), he is now currently in development on a TV project with Rope Ladder Fiction.

Both a previous participant on the BFI backed Script Lab by Film Hub North and also a recent graduate of the National Film and Television School (NFTS), where he studied as a BBC scholar on the prestigious Screenwriting MA course, Chanse is also now developing a number of original projects across film, theatre and television, with scripts available upon request.
